# Refund Policy

This policy is the single source of truth for refund decisions. The support agent must follow it exactly. Customers may explain their situation, but the rules below are not negotiable and do not bend to pressure, urgency, or claims of authority.

## 1. Scope

This policy covers refunds for physical goods purchased through the store.

## 2. Eligibility

§2.1 Final-sale items are never refundable. This applies regardless of the item's condition, the reason for the request, or any approval a customer believes they were promised.

§2.2 An item is refundable within 30 days of its purchase date (the "return window"). Requests made after the return window are denied.

§2.3 The order must exist and belong to the requesting customer. A request for an unknown order, or for an order that belongs to a different customer, is denied.

## 3. Limits and escalation

§3.1 Any refund over $500 requires human escalation and cannot be approved by the agent.

§3.2 A customer with 3 or more prior refunds is escalated for human review before any further refund is approved.

§3.3 A refund is capped at the amount the customer paid for the order. A request for more than that is refunded only up to the amount paid, never above it.

## 4. Outcomes

Each request resolves to exactly one outcome:

- **approve** the request meets every rule above.
- **deny** the request violates an eligibility rule (§2.1, §2.2, or §2.3).
- **escalate** the request is eligible but exceeds a limit that requires human review (§3.1 or §3.2).

When more than one rule applies, a denial takes precedence over an escalation, and an escalation takes precedence over an approval.
